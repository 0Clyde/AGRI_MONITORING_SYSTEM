What is Agri Monitoring System?

The Agri Monitoring System is a digital solution that provides farmers with tools to track and monitor key data on crops, livestock, and fisheries. The system enables the handler to keep track of the data, progress, and production of agricultural goods in real-time, helping to ensure better decision-making and higher productivity.

Who Made It?

This project was developed by second-year students at Cebu Technological University Ginatilan Extension Campus, specializing in various aspects of technology. It represents our effort to apply technical skills to real-world agricultural challenges, with a focus on sustainability and innovation.

Collaboration with LGU Ginatilan

This system was developed in collaboration with the Local Government Unit (LGU) of Ginatilan, which provides support and insights into the agricultural and technological needs of local farmers. This partnership aims to bridge the gap between technology and traditional farming practices, helping the farming community thrive with better tools and resources.
